Where to find the Reporting Key

Ask the customer to open the Axon console, switch to the correct account, then go to the Keys page and copy the value from the Reporting Key field.

  1. Open the account switcher in the top-right corner and select the target account.
  2. Enter `Account settings`.
  3. In the left navigation, click `Keys`.
  4. Copy the `Reporting Key` shown on that page and paste it here.
